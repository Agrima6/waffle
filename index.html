Our Story

From hustle to hotspot.

The Waffle Busters was built with ambition, experimentation and relentless energy. What started as a dream to create a standout dessert brand grew into a flavour-led experience that people remember, photograph and talk about.

We believe dessert should never feel ordinary. It should feel rich, indulgent, stylish and exciting. That’s why every section of the menu — from waffle sandwiches to bubble waffles, pancakes and coffees — is shaped around impact, visual drama and repeat cravings.
