About The Waffle Busters

The Waffle Busters is more than a dessert outlet. It is a flavour-first brand built around indulgence, strong visual appeal and memorable experiences that keep people coming back.

From Belgian-style waffles and bubble waffles to shakes, pancakes and coffee pairings, the goal is simple — make dessert feel exciting, premium and worth sharing.
